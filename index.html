Welcome to Ionia Management

We operate a fleet of motor tankers engaged in international trading. Established almost 30 years ago, the company operates from Piraeus and provides high quality services in an effective, efficient, safe and environmentally friendly manner.  

Global demand for oil, petroleum products and chemicals has been increasing steadily and have been the cornerstone of the world’s evolution. We pride ourselves in participating in such a dynamic industry continuing our tradition and assisting the world grow. 

Our goal is to meet and exceed the expectations of our clients and industry standards in terms of environmental awareness, safety and quality service.

 

We participate as a reliable corporate citizen and partner
in the international maritime community

HISTORY

1992

The Company was founded in 1992

1994

Having a rich background with the operation of small chemical tankers, Ionia moved into the management of medium-size tankers (about 40,000 dwt) in 1994.

1997

Took management of the first panamax tanker, expanding into the LR1 tanker sector becoming a founding member of the Star Tankers Panamax Pool.

2003

In August 2003, took management of its first ever newbuilding vessel, the 73,000 dwt “THEO T”. Gradually replacing the fleet under management with modern units.

2015

Ionia making history by exporting the first crude cargo to leave the USA after a 40 year export ban. 

TODAY

Today, the aggregate deadweight of the managed fleet exceeds 350,000 tonnes with a target to double the size of fleet under management in the coming years.
